# Muzgram — Chicago Metro Muslim Community Map

> Last updated: 2026-04-21
> Purpose: Complete reference for seed data planning, sales outreach, and neighborhood activation
> Coverage: City of Chicago + all suburbs (Cook, DuPage, Will, Kane, Lake, McHenry counties)

---

## How to Use This Document

Every neighborhood and suburb in Chicago metro with a meaningful Muslim population is listed here. For each area:

- **Population estimate** — approximate Muslim residents (not verified census, community-sourced)
- **Dominant ethnicity** — who is the primary Muslim community in this area
- **Halal density** — how many halal restaurants/businesses exist (Low / Medium / High)
- **Seed priority** — when to activate this area (1 = launch day, 2 = Month 1–3, 3 = Month 3–6)
- **Key mosques** — primary community institutions
- **Monetization type** — what products will sell best here
